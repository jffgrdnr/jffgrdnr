Testimonials

Small Facility

“We are saving $50 to $75 per week using Grove Menus in our small facilities. We also save time using this program. We just choose the food, and the shopping list generates itself. This gives us more time to take care of our residents. Giving the options for meals is a wonderful way to save; also you can change the item according to what may be on sale for that week.”
- Bonnie, Regional Manager for Assisted Living Franchise
“As a new small residential facility administrator, I was overwhelmed by the thought of menu planning and shopping. Fortunately, we use Grove Menu's and I found the system to be a wonderful tool. I can easily customize the menu to meet the needs and preferences of our residents. The shopping list is organized clearly by category, allowing me to go down an aisle and know exactly what I am picking up there. By doing the inventory, there is very little waste and no calls reporting missing items. The only thing stressful now is simply pushing full grocery carts! I had a few questions when first starting, but their staff was quick to e-mail me with all the information I needed. Thanks Grove Menus.”
- Jan, Administrator of 15 Bed Facility
